RE-FOLDR for Cubase
Auto-classify VST plugins and generate `PluginManager.xml` for Cubase.
→ Open App
---
What it does
Imports your Cubase Plugin Report (.txt) and PluginManager.xml, automatically sorts plugins into organized folders (EQ, Dynamics, Reverb, etc.), and exports a ready-to-use PluginManager.xml.
No installation. No server. Runs entirely in your browser.
How to use
Open the app link above
Drag & drop your Plugin Report .txt and PluginManager.xml onto the app
Review the auto-classified folders
Export and replace your PluginManager.xml
PluginManager.xml location:
Windows: `C:\Users\[username]\AppData\Roaming\Steinberg\Cubase [version]\PluginManager.xml`
Mac: `~/Library/Preferences/Cubase [version]/PluginManager.xml`
Compatibility
Tested with Cubase Pro 12, 13, 14.
